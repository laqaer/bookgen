# Product Strategy

## Operator Frame

This project is treated as an intellectual property pipeline:

Idea -> Manuscript -> Asset Stack -> Audience -> Revenue Engine

## Strategic Choice

- Current Mode: Self-publish + audience build first, then use traction for traditional leverage.
- Why this mode: builds direct audience ownership while preserving optionality for traditional publishing leverage.

## Success Metrics

- Manuscript quality: first draft complete with citation scaffolding and steelman objections.
- Audience readiness: pre-launch list growth and engagement.
- Conversion: sales + audiobook + newsletter conversion.
- Expansion: speaking and consulting pipeline activation.

## Risks and Controls

- Risk: overreach in claims.
- Control: boundary language + source placeholders + legal sensitivity pass.

- Risk: launch fragmentation.
- Control: one core narrative across proposal, metadata, and campaign assets.

- Risk: low post-launch monetization.
- Control: predesigned expansion products and offer ladder.
