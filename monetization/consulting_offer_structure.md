# Consulting Offer Structure

## Flagship Offer

Institutional Narrative Risk Audit

## Client Problem

Organizations need to communicate complex institutional realities without overclaiming, hedging into vagueness, or triggering avoidable credibility loss.

## Engagement Model

- Phase 1: diagnostic interviews and claim-map audit.
- Phase 2: messaging architecture with uncertainty protocols.
- Phase 3: rollout and measurement framework.

## Deliverables

- Claim boundary matrix
- Evidence confidence rubric
- Crisis communication decision tree
- 90-day implementation plan
