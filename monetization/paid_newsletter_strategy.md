# Paid Newsletter Strategy

## Positioning

Weekly briefings on institutional power, secrecy policy, media incentives, and accountability design.

## Content Stack

- One free weekly note (broad reach)
- One paid deep dive (framework + case analysis)
- One monthly office-hours/Q&A issue

## Conversion Ladder

1. Free excerpt content from Shadows and Structures
2. Structured framework posts
3. Paid subscription with toolkits and templates
4. Upsell into course and advisory products

## Retention Tactics

- publish corrections transparently
- run reader challenge prompts
- issue practical scorecards and checklists
