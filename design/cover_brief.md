# Cover Brief

## Title Lockup

- Title: Shadows and Structures
- Subtitle: State Secrecy, Bureaucratic Power, and the Limits of Public Knowledge
- Author: [Author Name]

## Art Direction

- Mood: institutional, shadowed, architectural, restrained.
- Visual metaphor: corridors, layered structures, partial light, records and ledgers.
- Avoid: thriller cliches, anonymous silhouette tropes, or conspiratorial iconography.

## Typography

- Primary title font: serious serif with strong print readability.
- Subtitle font: clean sans-serif with high contrast.
- Hierarchy must remain legible in thumbnail storefront contexts.

## Color Direction

- Charcoal base
- Slate gray structure elements
- Muted gold accent for authority and contrast

## Deliverables

- Full wrap print cover
- Ebook cover
- Audiobook square variant
- Social launch variants (portrait and landscape)
