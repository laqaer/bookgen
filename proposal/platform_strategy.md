# Platform Strategy

## Core Channels

- Email list (owned audience anchor)
- Substack-style serialized essays
- Podcast interviews in governance/media/policy niches
- Long-form social threads tied to chapter mechanisms

## Content Cadence

- Pre-launch: weekly thesis and excerpt notes.
- Launch window: 3-4 high-utility pieces per week.
- Post-launch: one deep analysis per week plus one practical framework piece.

## Partnership Targets

- Policy newsletters
- Civic organizations
- Journalism-adjacent communities
- University centers focused on governance and media

## Platform KPI Set

- Subscriber growth
- Open/click rates
- Preorder conversion
- Podcast referral conversion
