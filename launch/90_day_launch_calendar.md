# 90-Day Launch Calendar

## Days -90 to -61 (Foundation)

- Finalize thesis messaging and claim boundaries.
- Build lead magnet from one chapter framework.
- Start weekly newsletter cadence.
- Build podcast and media target list.

## Days -60 to -31 (Asset Build)

- Publish excerpt sequence.
- Collect ARC reader cohort.
- Draft and refine pitch templates.
- Confirm metadata and category strategy.

## Days -30 to -8 (Activation)

- Intensify newsletter cadence and social thread schedule.
- Distribute ARC reminders and review prompts.
- Lock podcast bookings around launch window.
- Publish op-ed style extract.

## Days -7 to +7 (Launch Week)

- Coordinate email launch sequence.
- Publish synchronized thread + essay + podcast clips.
- Trigger review-request sequence.
- Track daily conversion metrics and adapt messaging.

## Days +8 to +30 (Sustain)

- Publish reader objection responses.
- Release tactical frameworks from chapters.
- Push audiobook and speaking angle.
- Evaluate pricing and metadata adjustments.

## Days +31 to +90 (Expansion)

- Launch course waitlist.
- Run institutional outreach for consulting conversations.
- Package speaking topics from strongest-performing content.
