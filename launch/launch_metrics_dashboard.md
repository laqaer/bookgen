# Launch Metrics Dashboard (Template)

## Topline KPIs

- Email list growth (weekly)
- Open rate / click-through rate
- Preorder and unit sales by format
- Conversion by channel (email, podcast, social, partners)
- ARC review rate and average rating trend

## Mid-Funnel KPIs

- Landing page conversion
- Thread engagement-to-click ratio
- Podcast referral sessions
- Substack post saves and forwards

## Expansion KPIs

- Speaking inquiries
- Consulting discovery calls
- Newsletter paid conversion
- Course waitlist growth

## Review Rhythm

- Daily in launch week
- Twice weekly in first 30 days
- Weekly from day 31 onward
