# OUTLINE

## Book Thesis

- The project rejects unified-cabal narratives and argues that secrecy and bureaucratic power operate through documented institutional structures.
- Public knowledge limits are best explained by incentive design, legal authority, and information asymmetry.
- Reform requires durable oversight capacity, not one-off revelations.
