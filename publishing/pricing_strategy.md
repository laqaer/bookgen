# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

- Optimize for credibility + accessibility + sustainable unit economics.

## Guidance

- Ebook: lower-friction entry price to increase adoption.
- Paperback: standard trade nonfiction range with margin coverage.
- Hardback: premium positioning for institutional buyers.
- Audiobook: price to support perceived value and narrator quality.

## Iteration Rules

- Re-evaluate 30, 60, and 90 days post-launch.
- Test price changes only with controlled timing and clear KPI tracking.
