# ISBN Strategy

## Core Principle

Use separate ISBNs for each print format; ebook ISBN optional depending on distribution strategy.

## Recommended Allocation

- Paperback ISBN
- Hardback ISBN (if produced)
- Large-print ISBN (optional)
- Audiobook identifier workflow via distributor systems

## Governance

- Maintain a central metadata registry sheet.
- Ensure imprint consistency across platforms.
- Keep edition/version history for future updates.
